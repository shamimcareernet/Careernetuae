Case Study: Leading fashion e-commerce company

The Challenge

  • 80–100 roles to be filled in 6–7 months
  • Strong pedigree talent needed from top e-commerce/scaled startups
  • Roles: Backend, Frontend, Data Science, Analytics, SRE/DevOps
  • Levels: SE, SSE, TL, EM, Architect
  • Internal hiring process delays, no dedicated onsite TA support

Our Solutions

  • RPO model with 2 onsite resources + 1 backend delivery manager
  • 8–10 backend recruiters dedicated to the project
  • Close coordination with stakeholders; process gap bridging
  • Sourced via tech talent network; weekend drives for closures
  • Coffee-chat sessions post-offer to improve joining

The Impact

  • 105 offers with 70 onboardings in 7–8 months
  • 70% reduction in time-to-hire
  • Closed all active roles, exceeded hiring targets
